Cinematographers Cut 2007
The second event under the CinemArt banner: ‘The Cinematographer Cut’ was held at Mehboob Studies in November 2007 and was presented by Pixion.
The Cinematographers Night celebrated the four cinematographers who have changed the face of Indian filmmaking. The four cinematographers being honored were Ravichandran, Kiran Doehans, Binod Pradhan and V Manikandan.
The audience, comprising of other cinematographers and students from filmmaking schools and academies, watched as these four greats were introduced, honored and brought on stage by host Rohit Roy.  The night was all about celebrating the life and work of these four great men whose visual style has been pioneering in the history of Indian cinema. Through the night, AV’s featuring their stories and great moments behind the camera as well as conversations discussing their humble beginnings and career breaks ensued.
Joining them on stage were directors who each had worked with their cameraman, from Ashutosh Gowarikar supporting Kiran Deohans for Jodhaa Akbar, Farhan Akhtar coming for Ravichandran for Dil Chahta Hai, Rakesh Mehra supporting Binod Pradhan for Rang de Basanti and Farah Khan on an AV showering accolades on V Manikandan for Om Shanti Om and Main Hoon Na.

The next Cinematographers Night in 2008 will look at celebrating other great names of Indian filmmaking like Ashok Mehta, Santosh Sivan, Sudip Chatterjee and many others.
