FUE has developed an original property known as CinemArt, which looks at celebrating and honoring the best in behind the scenes of the Indian film industry. While actors and actresses steal the thunder at award shows, the work of those behind the scenes barely goes applauded. The idea of CinemArt is to create a space to not just honor, but also give a voice and platform to those individuals without whose contribution a frame of a film, a design of a dress or a note of music would be incomplete.

The brand aims to cut away from the loud noise of awards and ceremonies, placing emphasis on the work and substance that goes behind the scenes in putting together an entire film project. Without giving away awards or prizes, it highlights the importance of the work and contribution of various facets of the film industry, while also creating a database of information and knowledge compiled with the aid of the directors, cinematographers, choreographers etc for students and aficionados of filmmaking and cinema.
