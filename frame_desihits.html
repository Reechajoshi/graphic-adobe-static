Acclaimed choreographer Vaibhavi Merchant who has made the likes of Big B and SRK dance to her instructions will be launching the fourth tour of her famous musical ‘The Merchants of Bollywood’ and is expected to travel across the world in the next nine months including Germany, Switzerland, Netherlands, Austria, London, Dubai, Turkey, Singapore, Malaysia and South Africa from November 2007.

 
 


The musical is loosely based on Merchant’s life as it follows the story of a young woman who rebels against her father, a classical Indian dancer, to follow her dream and make a name for herself in Bollywood.  It’s already been performed to packed houses in Australia, New Zealand, UK and the US in the last four years and has received critical acclaim from almost every theatre critic. A vibrant mix of color, excellent dance acts and brilliant music thanks to composers Salim-Suleiman the musical is a must watch for anyone who loves Bollywood!
