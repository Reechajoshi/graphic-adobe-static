  HILFIGER DENIM FALL/WINTER 2007 COLLECTION  
   
 

The Hilfiger Denim Collection for Fall/Winter 2007 is divided into three Fashion Groups – Heritage, Forest and Ocean – each of which draws inspiration from iconic periods in American and European history.

Heritage is inspired by the strong and simple lines of vintage work wear. Also drawing from the fashions of college sports, it is classic Americana, updated with a cleaner, fresher twist. Throughout the Heritage line, echoes of styles made famous by the most enduring American icons are present. For men, the brooding cool of James Dean; for women, the inspiration is the iconic glamour of Marilyn Monroe.

Forest takes a turn towards the magical and unfolds against a sweeping, cinematic backdrop. Inside a towering 18th-century chateau, set in the heart of a wintry European forest, a glittering party is in full swing. Icons from across the ages – from Louis XIV to Sofia Coppola – mingle to a soundtrack of ‘80s punk rock. Glamour and eclecticism are the essence. Historic costumes are refined and simplified. Quilting and damask jostle against velvet, lace and flashes of gold. Forest is where Tommy Hilfiger Denim meets decadence.

Ocean recalls a period of glorious change in the United States: the end of the Civil War and the celebratory mood that swept a nation. The functional uniforms of war are cast aside, replaced by glittering medals for men and pretty trims for women. The optimism of the age is caught in Ocean’s vintage-inspired cuts. Expressing the swagger of an emerging nation, Ocean is smart, bold and utterly timeless
