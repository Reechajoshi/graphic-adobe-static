The Murjani Group has the Master Licensee for the Tommy Hilfiger brand in India. The Murjani Group in turn has set up a sub licensing relationships with reputed Indian companies to launch various categories.

The structure of the Tommy Hilfiger business in India is as follows:

  
   
 
 

The Tommy Hilfiger brand was launched in India in April 2004. Tommy Hilfiger himself was in India to launch the brand.
It was a two city launch. (Delhi and Bangalore) The launch activities included store opening events and a fashion show in Delhi.

Tommy Hilfiger today is the only premium designer wear segment brand to have reached a national foot print successfully. It is present in 7 prominent cities with its flagship stores. Bombay / Delhi/ Bangalore / Chandigarh / Madras / Hyd / Calcutta. Ahmedabad being the latest entrant, taking the number of cities covered with flagship stores to 8.

Arvind Murjani Brands Pvt. Ltd is the sub licensee for Tommy Hilfiger Apparel, and manages apparel business thru the Tommy Hilfiger Flagship stores across the country. It is a 50:50 joint venture between Arvind Mills and the Murjani Group. The joint venture was formed in 2004 to establish the Tommy Hilfiger retail stores in India.

The business is in a phase of very rapid expansion with Ahmedabad being the latest addition in Tommy Hilfiger flagship store distribution in India.

Tommy Hilfiger is the only international designer brand to provide a complete wardrobe of lifestyle products to Indian consumers. Besides the international offer of apparel for men and women, it offers a wide range of accessories that include watches, fragrances, eyewear, leather goods, handbags, men’s underwear etc.
