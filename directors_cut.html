The first event under the CinemArt banner: ‘The Directors Cut Night’, Taj Lands End June 2007.

The brain-child of Flying Unicorn Entertainment The Directors Cut is an annual event which looks to bring together film directors on a common platform to discuss and celebrate various facets of Indian cinema. Without awards and without giving any one person a platform of authority, the event aims at bringing the directors community together to discuss issues and find solutions as one.
 Held on June 3rd 2007 at the Taj Lands End, the first event saw directors like Madhur Bhandarkar, Kunal Kohli, Anurag Kashyap, Anurag Basu, Sudhir Misra, Siddharth Anand, Sangeeth Sivan, Abbas Tyrewala, Vivek Agnihotri, and many other directors take centre stage as Javed Akhtar and Bhawana Somaya hosted the evening.
A cinematic museum from the Bollywood art collection of S M Ausaja graced the walls of the hotel ballroom, showcasing great moments through the history of Indian cinema. From Pyasa to Dilwale Dulhaniya Le Jayenge, the directors were treated to a visual spectacle of film history.
The directors spent an evening discussing the idea of the ‘directors cut’—their changing roles as filmmakers in the Indian entertainment industry, the challenges that lie ahead for them, and how ‘Bollywood’ is and can gain more recognition on the international arena.
The event was televised as a half-hour show on ZOOM TV.
